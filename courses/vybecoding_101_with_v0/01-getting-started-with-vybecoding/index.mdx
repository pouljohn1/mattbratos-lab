This module frames the **VybeCoding** experience, walks you through setup with **v0**, and guides you into your very first VybeCoding session.

## Module objectives

By the end of this module you will be able to:

- Describe the **VybeCoding mindset** and how it differs from traditional coding
- Explain the **flow and goals** of this 2 hour session
- Complete the **basic setup and onboarding** for v0
- Participate in a **guided VybeCoding exercise** from idea to prototype

## Chapters in this module

1. **Course flow and goals**  
   Get a clear picture of what VybeCoding is, how v0 fits in, and what we will accomplish together.

2. **Setup and onboarding**  
   Make sure everyone can access v0, navigate the interface, and understand core concepts.

3. **First VybeCoding session**  
   Run a structured but playful session where you build a small interactive experience with v0.

## How this module fits into the 2 hour session

This single module is designed to **fill one 2 hour lecture**:

- **Part 1 – Orientation (0–20 min)**: Chapter 1
- **Part 2 – Setup (20–40 min)**: Chapter 2
- **Part 3 – Hands-on VybeCoding (40–110 min)**: Chapter 3
- **Part 4 – Reflection & wrap-up (110–120 min)**: Built into Chapter 3

Instructors can adjust timing depending on class size and technical familiarity.
